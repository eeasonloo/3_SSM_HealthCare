Attentions:

1、You should not change your diet too much three days before the check-up. Try to ensure a light diet, avoid greasy, too sweet and too salty food, and avoid uneven overeating to avoid affecting the test results.

2、It is important to take good rest before the check-up, try not to do strenuous exercise and not to get emotional, and to ensure that you get enough sleep and adjust your physical condition.

3、Do not drink alcohol before the check-up. Alcohol can affect the accuracy of the test, such as causing abnormal results of triglycerides and transaminases, and interfering with many tests.

4、You should dress lightly and simply on the day of the examination. Women should also be careful not to wear dresses, pantyhose or accessories that may interfere with the examination.

5、It is important for ladies to avoid menstruation in case it interferes with the relevant screening tests.

6、If you have a prostate or gynaecological ultrasound, you will need to hold your urine. You can drink appropriate water before the test to ensure your bladder is full before proceeding.

7、If you have a gynaecological examination, it is important to note that you should not have conjugal sex before the examination as it can interfere with the vaginal examination and interfere with the results.

8、It is also not advisable to over-cleanse or use vaginal medication before a gynaecological examination to avoid over-cleaning or medication interfering with the results of the examination.

9、Cooperating positively with your doctor, keeping a good attitude and informing him/her of your true condition will help him/her to help determine your health status.
