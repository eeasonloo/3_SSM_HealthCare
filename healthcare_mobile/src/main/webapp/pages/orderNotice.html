Appointment Guideline
Medical report: The medical report will be issued 5-7 working days after the medical examination.

A. Paper reports can be picked up after receiving a phone call from the medical examination centre, or registered at the reception desk on the day of the medical examination and requested to be sent by post (postage to be paid)

Before the medical examination:

1、Please eat a light diet the day before the examination, do not drink alcohol and do not work hard. On the day of the examination, please fast on an empty stomach.

2、Take rest the day before the medical examination and do not eat after 8pm. Avoid strenuous exercise and emotional excitement and ensure sufficient sleep to avoid affecting the results of the physical examination.

3、Gynecological and urine tests should not be done during the period of vacation.

When the medical examination:

1、Fasting tests are required for blood sampling, abdominal ultrasound, digital gastrointestinal, gastroscopy and other medical tests as indicated.

2、Please do not urinate during the ultrasound of the bladder, uterus and adnexa. If you do not urinate, you should drink water until your bladder is full. You should empty your urine before having a gynaecological examination.

3、Unmarried women will not have gynaecological examinations; pregnant women are advised to inform the medical staff in advance that radiological and other influential examinations will not be arranged.

4、Please remove any gold, silver, jade or other jewellery you are wearing before the radiological examination.

5、For MRI examinations, metal objects such as jewellery, watches, pagers and mobile phones should not be worn and magnetic cards should not be brought into the examination room to prevent demagnetisation.

After the medical examination:：

1、When you have completed all the items, please make sure you hand in your medical examination slip to the reception desk.

2、Please listen carefully to your doctor's advice and keep your check-ups up to date. Follow up or further examination and treatment.

3、Please keep the results of your check-ups for comparison with your next check-up and as information for your doctor's visit.
